About Tata Hospital

Committed to Excellence in Healthcare Since 1998

Welcome to Tata Medical Hospital

Tata Medical Hospital has been at the forefront of healthcare excellence for over 25 years. Founded in 1998 with a vision to provide world-class medical services to our community, we have grown to become one of the most trusted healthcare institutions in the region.

Our state-of-the-art facility spans across 5 floors with over 200 beds, equipped with the latest medical technology and staffed by more than 150 highly qualified healthcare professionals. We are committed to delivering patient-centered care that combines medical expertise with compassion and dignity.

At MediCare, we believe that quality healthcare is a right, not a privilege. Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and advanced medical care to all sections of society, ensuring that every patient receives the attention and treatment they deserve.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, high-quality healthcare services that improve the health and well-being of our community. We strive to be leaders in medical innovation while maintaining the highest standards of patient care, safety, and satisfaction.

We are committed to continuous improvement in our services, embracing new technologies and treatment methods that enhance patient outcomes. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that every patient receives personalized care tailored to their unique needs.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred healthcare provider in the region, recognized for our clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and patient-first approach. We envision a future where advanced medical care is accessible to everyone.

Our vision extends beyond treatment to prevention, education, and community health promotion. We aim to create a healthier society through proactive healthcare initiatives and partnerships with community organizations.
